Receive

The receive tab allows you to receive funds. Displayed prominently is the mwcmq address that other users can use to send funds to you.

Your wallet will need to be online at the time that they send funds to you. Also, account information like funds that are available and awaiting confirmation is displayed on this page.

Indicators of connectivity to the keybase and mwcmq listener are also displayed on this page.
