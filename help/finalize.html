Finalize

This tab allows you to finalize and publish file based or Slatepack transaction. When you complete this step, the transaction will be published to the MWC blockchain, your coins will be sent, and this operation will not be reversible. Please validate the amount and receiver address before confirm finalization and submit.

Your wallet need to be connected to the node that is healty. The node is healty if it is online and have enough peers, so your transaction will be able to reach the mining pool nodes.

Indicators of connectivity for the MWC node is displayed at the bottom of this page. Green indicator signals that your wallet connected to the MWC network.
